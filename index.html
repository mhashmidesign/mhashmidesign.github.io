IRIS Customer Portal Redesign

Nexus Vehicle Rental

As the Lead UI/UX Designer at Nexus Vehicle Rental, I was responsible for redesigning the IRIS platform to address problems with increasing bounce rates, lack of bookings by mobile devices, and a need to improve the customer user experience and ensure WCAG2.1 compliance and responsiveness across all devices. I worked closely with the in-house development team and conducted user research and testing to inform the design of the platform. I was also responsible for managing the design system and leading the visual design for the platform.

Nexus E

Nexus Vehicle Rental

Nexus E is an electric vehicle (EV) marketplace and contactless charging portal that offers businesses a comprehensive solution for managing their EV fleet and charging needs. With over 20,000+ charge points and more than 20+ providers. My role was to lead the full UI/UX strategy for the product. I worked closely with the wider business, stakeholders and partners to design and develop the user interface and user experience of the Nexus E, specifically for the electric vehicle marketplace and contactless charging portal.
